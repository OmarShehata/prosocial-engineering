Prosocial Engineering - Active Projects

  • 🧢 The Public Defender is a social media character that facilitates good faith public debate.
  • ⚖️ Anatomy of an Internet Argument is a series that features successful resolution of internet arguments & collects examples of people changing their mind in the real world. It's meant to be a game that anyone can play, towards improving the default culture of public discourse. See the vision described a bit more here and in this comment by Shadow Rebbe.
  • 🔬 ORI (website, twitter) is a project for designing & maintaining the optimal set of social norms for accelerating research & truth seeking.
