Prosocial Engineering is an open source intelligence agency.

We do everything that intelligence agencies do (monitoring, infiltrating, influencing), but our target always knows what we're doing before we do it. Our "infiltration" technique is: knocking on your door.

Our primary purpose is sovereignty of mind. We make money by selling services & creating products that contribute to this purpose. Some of our notable work includes:

  • ​ Twitter Community Archive - a first of its kind "open culture research weather station"
  • ​Anatomy of an Internet Argument - an open source psyop to make internet debates between strangers wholesome (or at least productive)
  • ​Good & Evil Words - a prototype that demonstrates what monitoring & social engineering of collective understanding on a mass scale can look like

We consider our work complete when any two nodes on earth pass the "two-way valve" test: when group A has a message that group B wants to receive, then the information flows freely. This is what we call the hypothetical Ideal Information Information.
